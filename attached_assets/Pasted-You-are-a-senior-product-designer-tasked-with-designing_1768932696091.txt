### DESIGN DIRECTIVES (High-Priority)

**1) Home / Intent Screen**  
Focus on **Kanso** and **Seijaku**:
- Clean, uncluttered view with generous negative space.  
- Only the essential ‚Äî destination input and a tiny, subdued üí¨ button for optional user notes.

- Layout should be slightly **off-center** (Fukinsei), e.g., the search input subtly shifted slightly left of center for organic balance.

**2) Colors & Textures**  
Apply **Shibui** and **Shizen**:
- Earthy, muted palette (soft beiges, misty grays, muted greens).  
- Introduce extremely subtle background texture (e.g., light paper grain) so full white is never flat or harsh.  
- Avoid high-contrast blacks and whites.  

**3) Typography & Elements**  
Reflect **Kanso** with humanist text:
- Use a warm, humanist sans typeface (or gentle serif if it feels more tactile).  
- Very generous line spacing; avoid condensed typography.  
- Slightly imperfect iconography (hand-drawn or organic stroke).  

**4) Spatial Composition**  
Embed **Fukinsei** and **Seijaku** in layout:
- Headers, inputs, and controls should have generous padding and not adhere to rigid grid lines.  
- Slight offsets ‚Äî e.g., buttons not perfectly aligned on the same axis ‚Äî to create a natural rhythm.

**5) Motion & Interaction**

- Very subtle fade-in and easing animations rather than sharp transitions.
